# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in the Traceloom project should be respectful, technically honest, and collaborative.
We expect contributors to debate ideas rigorously without personal attacks, harassment, or exclusionary behavior.

## Expected behavior

- communicate clearly and in good faith
- focus critique on code, design, evidence, and user impact
- respect different levels of experience and language background
- accept correction when shown clear technical or behavioral issues

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- sexualized language or unwelcome personal attention
- doxxing or publishing private information without consent
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads
- knowingly misrepresenting test results, benchmarks, or security impact

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to `bikeread2008@gmail.com`.
If the report involves private repository or security material, say so in the subject line.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
Enforcement decisions will aim to protect participants and keep the project usable for collaboration.
